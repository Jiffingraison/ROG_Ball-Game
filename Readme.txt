Welcome to Road to Glory!
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Players must make their way through two difficult stages in this thrilling ball game, each with its own gameplay. In Level 1, you take control of a football and must gather two keys and coins to advance to the next level. The game will end and you will have to start over if you fall off the ground or collide into an enemy. You can't finish the level unless you've collected both keys. The action is the same in Level 2, but the gameplay is more harder and you control a basketball rather than a football. Players of all skill levels will find this game to be entertaining and interesting due to its straightforward principles and captivating challenges.
